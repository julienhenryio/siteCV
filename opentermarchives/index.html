Pinterest removes artistic and educational nudity exceptions from Community Guidelines

Pinterest • Community Guidelines • June 6, 2025
By Julien Henry, a third-party contributor

Pinterest removed exceptions that previously allowed nudity in artistic, educational, and medical contexts from its Community Guidelines. The platform deleted an entire provision that permitted "some nudity in certain contexts," including nudity in paintings and sculptures, science and historical contexts, and content about breastfeeding and mastectomies.

The policy now prohibits all "nudity" without qualification, whereas it previously banned "most nudity." This change eliminates Pinterest's previous recognition of contextual differences for educational, artistic, or medical content.

This represents a significant tightening of content moderation policies, potentially affecting educational institutions, art communities, and health-related content creators who previously relied on these contextual exceptions.
